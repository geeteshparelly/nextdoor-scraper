Our Story

Crafted with Intention

Austin Candle Co. started in 2019 when I got frustrated with mass-produced candles full of synthetic junk. I wanted candles that actually smelled good, burned clean, and were made with care.

Today, every candle is still hand-poured by me in our East Austin studio. We use 100% soy wax, cotton wicks, and premium fragrance oils. No additives, no shortcuts.

Emma Rodriguez

Founder & Maker
