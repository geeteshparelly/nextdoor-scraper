Our Story

Four Generations of Fruit

The Caskey family has been growing fruit in Central Texas since 1952. What started as a small peach orchard has grown into 80 acres of stone fruit, citrus, and berries.

We still pick everything by hand and bring it to market within 24 hours of harvest. That's freshness you can taste.
