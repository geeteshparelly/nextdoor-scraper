Our Story

High Altitude, High Quality

At 3,500 feet in the Texas Panhandle, our orchard experiences the cold nights that apples need to develop their signature crunch and sweetness.

We've been growing apples for three generations, and we make the drive to Austin every weekend during apple season to share our harvest.
