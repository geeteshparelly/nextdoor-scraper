Our Story

Jane's Bountiful Pantry began in 2012 with a simple vision: a place where gluten-free and dairy-free pastries could be enjoyed without sacrificing texture or taste.

We believe that being gluten-free and dairy-free should be delicious AND healthy. Every recipe has been perfected over years of baking and feedback from our wonderful farmers market customers.
