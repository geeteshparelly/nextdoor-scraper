Our Story

Made With Intention

Khôra — from the Greek word for "space" — began as a small studio practice in East Austin. What started as a meditation became a mission: to create objects that hold space for the moments that matter.

Every piece in our collection is made by hand, from wheel-thrown ceramics to naturally-dyed textiles. We believe in slow craft, sustainable materials, and the beauty of imperfection.

Find us at Barton Creek Farmers Market every Saturday, or schedule a studio visit to see where it all begins.
