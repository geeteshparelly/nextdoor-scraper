Our Heritage

Roots Run Deep

Zaytoun — Arabic for "olive" — represents more than a product. It's a connection to land, family, and tradition. Our olive oil comes from groves in the hills of Palestine, some trees over 1,000 years old, tended by the same families for generations.

We work directly with Palestinian farmers to bring their harvest to Texas. Each bottle is cold-pressed within hours of picking, preserving the vibrant green color and complex flavors that make Palestinian olive oil legendary.

When you buy Zaytoun, you support small farmers, preserve ancient groves, and taste history in every drop.
