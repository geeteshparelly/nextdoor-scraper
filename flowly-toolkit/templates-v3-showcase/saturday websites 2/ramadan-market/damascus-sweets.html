Our Story

Recipes From Home

Damascus Sweets began in our family kitchen. When we came to Austin from Syria, we brought with us the recipes that had been passed down through four generations — recipes perfected in the souks of Damascus.

Every piece of baklava is hand-layered. Every kunafa is stretched fresh. We use real butter, pure rose water, and premium nuts because that's how our grandmother taught us. No shortcuts, no substitutes.

Whether you're celebrating Eid, hosting iftar, or just craving something sweet — we're here to share the flavors of home.
