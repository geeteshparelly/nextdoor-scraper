Our Story

Flavors From Home

Sahara Spice House was born from homesickness. When our family moved to Austin, we missed the flavors we grew up with — the za'atar from grandmother's kitchen, the saffron from the markets of Tehran, the sumac picked from wild bushes.

We started sourcing spices from the same farms and markets we remembered, and now we share those flavors with you. Every spice is hand-selected, freshly ground, and blended with recipes passed down through generations.
