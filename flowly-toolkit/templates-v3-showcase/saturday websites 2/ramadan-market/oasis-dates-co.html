Our Story

From Palm to Table

Oasis Dates Co was founded with a simple mission: bring the finest quality dates to Austin. We source our Medjool dates directly from family farms in California's Coachella Valley, where the desert climate produces the world's best dates.

Every date is hand-selected for size, sweetness, and texture. We stuff them by hand with premium nuts, dip them in artisan chocolate, and create treats that honor this ancient fruit.

Whether you're breaking fast during Ramadan, hosting a gathering, or just craving something naturally sweet — we've got you.
