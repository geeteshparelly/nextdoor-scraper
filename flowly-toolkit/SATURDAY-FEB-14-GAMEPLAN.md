- Multiple food stands
- Very diverse, working-class market
- Cash preferred by many vendors

### Vendor Types Present
- **Vintage/antique dealers**
- **Clothing sellers** (new and used)
- **Electronics/phone accessories**
- **Tools and hardware**
- **Food vendors** (tacos, drinks, snacks)
- **Jewelry and accessories**
- **Home goods**
- **Auto parts**
- **Amazon pallet resellers**
- **Western wear**
- **Toys and collectibles**

### Best Prospects at Austin Country Flea
Look for vendors who are clearly running a REAL BUSINESS:
- **Food vendors with permanent stalls** (tacos, BBQ, etc.)
- **Specialty retailers** (western wear, boots, jewelry)
- **Tool/equipment sellers**
- **Auto parts vendors**
