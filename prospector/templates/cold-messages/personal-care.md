## Personalization Tips for Personal Care

- **Salons:** Focus on stylist portfolios, service menus, booking
- **Barbers:** Before/after photos, walk-in vs appointment info
- **Nail techs:** Design galleries, nail art portfolio
- **Estheticians:** Service descriptions, skincare focus
- **Makeup artists:** Bridal/event galleries, booking for events
- **Tattoo artists:** Portfolio is EVERYTHING, style showcase
- **Massage/Spa:** Relaxation imagery, service packages, gift cards
